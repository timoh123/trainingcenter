What are We?

>About Us

Genius computer training center is a small institution founded by some young ambitious, passionate Cameroonian who had the dreams of increasing computer literacy in their community and country. These young Cameroonian were determine to see their fellow Cameroonian become computer littrates by transmitting their savior fair to everyone who is determine to learn. GCTC offers basic computer courses like MS OFFICE( MS Word, MS Excel, MS Access, MS Publisher, and Power point), Graphic Design, PhotoShop, Web Developement ( Html, Css, Javascript) and more. See our traning section to find out the best training that suits you. We have also adopt a modern system of learning that we believe is best for all and also giving our students access to the best E-learning plartforms and also international career opportunities

>Our Mission

Genius Computer Training (GCTC) Is an institution thats was created by young individuals not mainly for profitability but also helps the young generation and thoes who cant afford expensive training centers. To achieve these Goals our team orgarnises Computer holiday classes where we trained both children and aldults on some important bases of computer at very low cost. our team also work togather with IDP Cenetrs also to give the best training to our brothers and sisters affected by the on going crises in the northwest & southwest regions of our country. .

>Our Vision

Genius computer trainning has the vision of seeing the rate of computer literacy increase in cameroon and also give the possibility to those who can not afford expensive university institution and training centers to get the best best of our training programs and also help meet thier career dreams . we also have the vision of impacting young Cameroonian by by giving them access to best career opportunities.
